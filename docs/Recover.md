
# **Recover**

**Recover** helps subscription companies reduce accidental customer churn and **boost top-line ARR by 10+%** using **machine learning** to automate the recovery of failed payments. The system retries failed payments to rescue transactions and customers that would have been lost due to accidental payment failure.

!!! note "Info"

    For a typical $100M subscription business, Butter finds **$10M in failed payments** and keeps **hundreds of thousands of users connected with the products and services they love**.
